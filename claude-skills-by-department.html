What’s a Claude skill? A small, reusable instruction pack that teaches Claude to do one job well. To use one: open its link, download the skill folder, and add it in Claude (Settings → Capabilities → Skills, or drop the folder into your project) — then just ask Claude to do that task.

These are real, already-built Claude skills. Click More info on any card for a fuller description; the source link opens the exact skill. Skills that serve more than one team appear under each (e.g. the RFP responder shows in Sales, Marketing, and Legal).
